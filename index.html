The Heritage of Amma's Kitchen

RK Foods was born from a simple passion: to share the authentic, comforting flavors of home-cooked food that we grew up with.

Every package of our special spice powders, thick pickles, and traditional sweets is prepared by hand in small batches. We source premium, single-origin ingredients, dry them under the natural sun, and slow-roast them to lock in rich aroma and nutritional values.
